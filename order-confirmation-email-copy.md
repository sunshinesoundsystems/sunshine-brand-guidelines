## Usage Notes

**When to Use This Email:**
- Order/ticket purchase confirmation
- Immediately after payment processed
- Before ticket delivery email
- For paid events

**Do NOT Use For:**
- Free event RSVPs (use RSVP Confirmation email)
- Ticket delivery (use Ticket Confirmation email)
- Waitlist notifications (use Waitlist email)

**Brand Voice:**
- Direct and welcoming
- Confident
- Action-oriented
- Music/vibe-focused

**Tone Elements:**
- "You're in!" ‚Äì Direct confirmation
- "See you on the dance floor" ‚Äì Music scene language
- "Come ready to vibe" ‚Äì SSS attitude
- No greeting (no "Hey [Name]") ‚Äì more transactional
- Focus on the event, not the transaction

---

## Copy Differences from Other Emails

**vs RSVP Confirmation:**
- No personal greeting
- Doesn't mention location in main message
- More transactional

**vs Ticket Confirmation:**
- No ticket download button
- "EVENT DETAILS" vs "GRAB YOUR TICKETS"
- Confirms order, not ticket delivery
