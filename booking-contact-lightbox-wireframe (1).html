Form Submission & States
Submission Flow
1. Validation: Client-side validation, highlight errors in pink
2. Submit State: Button text → "SENDING...", show loading spinner
3. Success: Form replaced with success message + checkmark
4. Error: Show error message, allow retry

See booking-contact-lightbox-copy.md for complete error messages, success messages, and email templates.
