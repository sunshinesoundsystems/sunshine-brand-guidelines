04 ‚Äî Voice & Tone

How We Speak

Our voice is confident, energetic, and approachable. We speak like your friend who always knows where the best party is‚Äîknowledgeable but never pretentious, professional but never stuffy. We use language that's alive, direct, and full of personality.

Voice Characteristics

We Are

  • Energetic: "Turn up the volume!" not "Increase audio levels"
  • Direct: "Let's make magic together" not "We facilitate event experiences"
  • Confident: "Sound that hits different" not "Quality audio solutions"
  • Warm: "Your sound crew" not "Service providers"
  • Playful: "Big fun, any size" not "Scalable event solutions"
  • Authentic: "We obsess over sound" not "Committed to excellence"

We Avoid

  • Corporate jargon: "Solutions," "leverage," "synergy"
  • Overly technical: Unless explaining equipment specs
  • Passive voice: "Experiences are created" ‚Üí "We create experiences"
  • Generic claims: "Best in class" ‚Üí Show, don't tell
  • Stiff formality: Write like you speak
  • Tired clich√©s: "Taking it to the next level"

Typography Rules

  • Always use ALL CAPS for Anton: It's designed to be shouted
  • Never stretch or condense: Maintain proper aspect ratios
  • Generous line spacing: Use 1.8-2.0 line-height for body text
  • Contrast is key: Dark text on light backgrounds, light on dark
  • Hierarchy matters: Use size and weight to create clear visual order

Web Implementation

Important: Anton Regular (400 weight) from Google Fonts may render thinner than the logo on some browsers and operating systems. The logo appears bolder and more solid.

To make Anton appear bolder on the web to match the logo:

font-family: 'Anton', sans-serif;
font-weight: 400;
letter-spacing: -0.05em;
-webkit-text-stroke: 0.5px; (adds subtle weight)
text-rendering: optimizeLegibility;

Avenir requires a web font license or can be substituted with "Avenir Next" (system font on Apple devices) or "Montserrat" as a free alternative.
