# SSS Email Copy Guide

Complete copy reference for all Sunshine Sound Systems email communications.

---

## Brand Voice

**Casual & Welcoming**
- Conversational language
- Speak directly to the person
- Friendly but not overly familiar

**Confident & Assured**
- Direct statements ("You're in", "You're on the list")
- No hedging or uncertain language
- Action-oriented messaging

**Music/Vibe-Focused**
- Reference the experience, not just logistics
- Use scene language ("dancefloor", "grooves", "vibes")
- Emphasize atmosphere and community

---

## Standard Elements

**Signature Sign-off:**
Stay Golden,
Sunshine Sound Systems

**Contact Line:**
